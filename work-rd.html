Thesis · Circular Design · Manufacturing

Modularity in Lattice Structures for Circular Product Design

Delft University of Technology·Master's Thesis·Jan – Jun 2022·Delft, Netherlands

Explored modularity in lattice structures as a strategy for repairability and reuse in circular product design. The research focused on efficient manufacturing of parametric modular lattices through subtractive processes — investigating how structural geometry can support end-of-life disassembly without compromising mechanical performance.

Strategic Design · Sustainability · Circular Economy

Prometheus — Sustainable Future for EV Battery Lifecycles

Delft University of Technology·Strategic Design Research·Nov – Dec 2019·Delft, Netherlands

Strategic design research assessing the true environmental break-even of EVs versus combustion vehicles, and exploring circular economy approaches through battery repurposing before recycling. The project aimed to discover innovation sweet spots for the future end-of-life of EV batteries and design an effective solution minimising environmental impact.

User-Centred Design · Product Research

wind NOise — Ear Protection while Biking

Delft University of Technology·MSc Course Project·Feb 2020·Delft, Netherlands

The project applied UCD methods — user research, interaction analysis, and iterative prototyping — to arrive at a functional, ergonomic solution developed as part of the MSc Integrated Product Design programme at TU Delft.
