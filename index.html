I'mPranav Gawde

Product Designer·Mechanical Engineer·Startup Consultant

Mumbai, India

Designing products, designing programmes, and designing designers who design better.

Two ideologies anchor my work. The first: design is a long-term responsibility — products and services should hold up not just on the day they ship, but for everything that comes after. The second: less is more — the strongest solutions come from clarity and restraint, not from adding features.

Across nine years of building, those ideas show up in three different ways. As a designer and engineer, I work on the thing itself — sketches to CAD, prototypes to production. As a consultant and liaison, I sit alongside founders and institutions, helping ventures find their footing. As a researcher, I explore the problems before they have products.
